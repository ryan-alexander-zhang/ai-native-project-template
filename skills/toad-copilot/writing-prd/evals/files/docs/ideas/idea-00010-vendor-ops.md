# Idea Brief

## One-line Summary

A vendor quote comparison copilot that helps operations teams normalize quotes and spot the best option quickly.

## Problem

Vendor quotes arrive in inconsistent formats, which makes it slow to compare price, service level, and contract risk.

## Target User

Operations managers and procurement leads reviewing multiple vendor offers.

## Current Alternatives

Spreadsheets, manual note taking, and side-by-side PDF reading.

## Proposed Solution

Ingest quotes, normalize key fields, and surface a structured comparison with recommendation support.

## Why Better

It removes manual comparison work and makes tradeoffs easier to review.

## Why Now

Teams are receiving more vendor proposals while being asked to move faster on procurement decisions.

## Risks

- Quote formats may vary enough to make normalization unreliable at first.
